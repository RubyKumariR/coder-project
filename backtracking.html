N-Queens Problem

Best Case: O(n!)

Worst Case: O(n!)

The N-Queens Problem involves placing N queens on an N×N chessboard such that no two queens threaten each other.

Sudoku Solver

Best Case: O(9^k)

Worst Case: O(9^k)

The Sudoku Solver uses backtracking to fill a 9×9 grid such that each row, column, and 3×3 subgrid contains all digits from 1 to 9.

Important DSA Questions for FAANG
